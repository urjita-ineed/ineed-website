## 📝 Customization (Top Tasks)

### 1. Change Company Name
- Replace "ineed" with your name in all `.html` files
- Update CSS if needed

### 2. Change Colors
Edit `css/styles.css` lines 10-18:
```css
--primary-color: #000;    /* Your primary color */
--accent-color: #ff6b35;  /* Your accent color */
```

### 3. Update Contact Info
Replace globally:
- `ineed4322@gmail.com` → Your email
- `8169746346` → Your phone
- `123 Tech Street` → Your address

### 4. Add Logo
Replace text logo in navbar with image tag

### 5. Update Services
Modify services list in `index.html` and `services.html`
