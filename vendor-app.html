How the ineed Vendor App Works

Work on your own terms, accept orders freelance

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Step 1: Go Online

Download the ineed vendor app and complete your verification. When you're ready to work, simply tap "Go Online" to start receiving orders.

  • Verify your documents and ID
  • Set your service category
  • Turn on "Go Online" when ready
  • Work whenever you want

Step 2: Receive Notifications

When customers book your service, you receive real-time notifications showing customer details, location, and job description. You have complete freedom to choose.

  • Real-time order notifications
  • Customer name and ratings
  • Service location and details
  • Estimated job time

Step 3: Accept or Decline

You decide whether to accept each order. If you're busy, unsure, or prefer another job - simply decline. There's no penalty, no pressure. It's completely flexible.

  • Review order details completely
  • Accept orders you want
  • Decline without penalty
  • No minimum acceptance rate required

Step 4: Navigate to Customer

Once you accept an order, get the customer's exact location with turn-by-turn navigation. Chat directly with the customer and coordinate arrival time.

  • Get customer's exact location
  • Turn-by-turn navigation
  • Direct messaging with customer
  • Share your ETA

Step 5: Complete Service

Arrive at the location, complete the service professionally, and mark it complete in the app. The customer confirms completion and the job is done.

  • Complete the service
  • Take before/after photos if needed
  • Mark service as complete
  • Get customer confirmation

Step 6: Get Paid Instantly

Earnings are credited instantly to your account. Withdraw anytime to your bank account. No waiting, no surprises - transparent pricing shown upfront.

  • Instant payment upon completion
  • View all earnings in real-time
  • Withdraw to bank anytime
  • Transparent pricing structure
