How the Vendor App Works

01

Go Online

Open the vendor app and set your status to "Online". You're now ready to receive orders from customers nearby.

02

Receive Order Notifications

When a customer books your service, you get a real-time notification with order details, customer location, and service requirements.

03

Accept or Decline

You have 30 seconds to review the order. Accept it if it works for you, or decline it without any penalty. The order goes to nearby professionals.

04

Navigate to Customer

Once you accept, get turn-by-turn navigation to the customer's location with real-time chat communication.

05

Complete Service

Provide quality service at the customer's location. Take photos/videos of completed work if required.

06

Get Paid Instantly

Payment is processed immediately after service completion. Funds reflect in your bank account within 24 hours.
