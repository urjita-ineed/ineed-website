How the ineed Customer App Works

Book services in 4 simple steps

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Step 1: Select Your Service

Choose the service you need from our available options. We offer Carpenter, Electrician, Painter, Plumber, CCTV Technician, and AC Technician services. Simply tap on your desired service category to begin your booking.

  • Browse available service categories
  • Carpenter, Electrician, Painter, Plumber, CCTV Technician, AC Technician
  • Select the service that matches your needs

Screenshot 1

Screenshot 2

Step 2: Choose Your Sub-Service

Each service category includes various specialized options tailored to your specific needs. Select the exact sub-service that matches your requirement to ensure you get the right professional for your job.

  • View available sub-services within your chosen category
  • Select the specific task you need help with
  • Get matched with the right specialist

Step 3: Select Booking Type

Choose whether you need immediate service or prefer to schedule your booking for a later date and time. Select the booking option that works best for your schedule and urgency.

  • Select "Book Immediately" for urgent service
  • Or choose "Schedule Booking" for a later date
  • Flexible options for your convenience

Screenshot 3

Screenshot 4

Step 4: Enter Details & Confirm Booking

Provide all the required information for your service booking. Enter your preferred date and time, specify how many vendors you'd like to receive (up to 5 options), select your service address, and confirm your order.

  • Enter preferred date and time
  • Select number of vendors (up to 5)
  • Choose or enter your service address
  • Review your details and click "Order Booked"
