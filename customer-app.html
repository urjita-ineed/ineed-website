How the ineed Customer App Works

Book services in 4 simple steps

Step-by-Step Walkthrough

Step 1: Select Service & Time

Choose the service you need - plumber, electrician, CCTV technician, AC technician, painter or carpenter. Select whether you want immediate booking or to schedule for later.

  • Pick your service category
  • Choose "Book Now" for immediate service
  • Or "Schedule" to pick date and time
  • Provide location details

Screenshot 2

Step 2: Get 5 Vendor Options

After entering your details, ineed shows you up to 5 available vendors nearby. Each vendor card shows their rating, reviews, and experience.

  • See 5 nearby vendors
  • Check star ratings from real customers
  • Read vendor reviews
  • Compare experience and pricing

Step 3: Choose Your Vendor

Pick the vendor you prefer based on ratings, reviews, and experience. You have complete control over who gets assigned to your service.

  • Read detailed vendor profiles
  • Check customer reviews and ratings
  • Choose the vendor you trust
  • Confirm your booking

Screenshot 4

Step 4: Track & Complete Service

Once your vendor confirms, track them in real-time. They'll arrive at your location to complete the service. Pay after completion through your preferred method.

  • Real-time vendor tracking
  • Direct chat with vendor
  • Get service completion confirmation
  • Rate and review the service
